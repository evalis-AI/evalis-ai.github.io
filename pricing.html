❓ FAQ

Pricing Questions

Do you charge upfront or after delivery? ▼
We typically work with a 50% advance and 50% on delivery model. For larger projects, we offer milestone-based payments (e.g., 30-30-40). Enterprise clients can discuss NET-30 terms.
Are there any hidden fees? ▼
Absolutely not. The price we quote is the price you pay. Third-party costs like hosting (AWS, Vercel), domain names, API subscriptions, or app store fees are separate and clearly communicated upfront.
What if my project needs more features later? ▼
We offer maintenance contracts and can add features anytime at agreed hourly rates. All our code is well-documented and extensible — you're never locked in.
Do you offer refunds? ▼
If we haven't started development, we offer a full refund. Once development begins, we don't offer refunds — but we do guarantee revisions until you're satisfied with the deliverable.
What payment methods do you accept? ▼
We accept bank transfer (SWIFT), PayPal, Wise, Payoneer, and Stripe. All invoices are in USD. We work with clients across the US, UK, UAE, Europe, Australia, and worldwide.
What timezone do you work in? ▼
Our core hours are IST (UTC+5:30), but we regularly overlap with US, UK, and Middle East timezones. We're flexible and async-friendly — communication never suffers regardless of your location.

❓ FAQ

Common Questions

How accurate is the extraction?▼
After training on your specific document formats, we achieve 95–99% accuracy. A human review step can be added for critical data points that require 100% accuracy.
Can it handle handwritten documents?▼
Yes. Our OCR handles printed text, typed text, and handwritten text. Accuracy for handwriting depends on legibility but typically exceeds 90% for clear handwriting.
What file formats are supported?▼
PDF, PNG, JPG, TIFF, DOCX, and scanned documents. We also handle photos taken from mobile phones — our preprocessing normalizes skew, contrast, and resolution.
Is my data secure?▼
All documents are processed in encrypted pipelines. Data is never stored permanently — processed results are pushed to YOUR system and source documents are deleted. We sign NDAs for sensitive data.

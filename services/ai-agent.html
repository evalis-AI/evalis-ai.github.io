❓ FAQ

Common Questions

What is a custom AI agent?▼
A custom AI agent is a chatbot trained specifically on your business data — your products, services, FAQs, and pricing. It lives on your website and answers customer questions 24/7 with accurate, context-aware responses. Unlike generic chatbots, it knows YOUR business inside out.
How is this different from ChatGPT or other AI tools?▼
ChatGPT is a general-purpose AI. Your custom agent is trained exclusively on YOUR data — it only answers questions about YOUR business. It's fully branded, embedded on your site, captures leads, and has analytics. It's a product, not a chat window.
Can I update the agent's knowledge later?▼
Absolutely. You can update your agent's knowledge base anytime — add new products, change pricing, update policies. Changes are reflected within minutes.
Does it work on mobile?▼
Yes. The agent widget is fully responsive and works beautifully on desktop, tablet, and mobile. Voice input is supported on Chrome and Edge browsers.
What if the AI gives a wrong answer?▼
Since your agent is trained only on your verified data (RAG architecture), hallucinations are minimal. When it doesn't know something, it gracefully says so and offers to connect the user with a human. You can review all conversations in the analytics dashboard.

❓ FAQ

Common Questions

Is this just another cold email tool?▼
No. Cold email tools send templates. Our AI agent actually researches each prospect, understands their business challenges, and writes truly personalized messages. It's the difference between a mass mailer and a thoughtful SDR.
Will my emails end up in spam?▼
We set up proper email authentication (SPF, DKIM, DMARC), use warm-up sequences, and send at human-like intervals. Combined with personalized content, deliverability stays above 95%.
How many leads will I get per month?▼
Depends on your ICP and industry, but typical campaigns generate 50–200 qualified leads per month, with 5–15% positive reply rates (vs 1–2% for generic cold email).
Can I review messages before they're sent?▼
Yes! You can set the agent to "approval mode" where you review and approve each batch before sending. Or let it run fully autonomous once you're comfortable with the messaging quality.

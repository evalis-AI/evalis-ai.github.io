❓ FAQ

Common Questions

How is GEO different from SEO?▼
SEO optimizes for search engine rankings (blue links). GEO optimizes for AI-generated answers — getting your brand cited when someone asks ChatGPT, Perplexity, or Google AI a question. Different algorithms, different strategies.
How long before I see results?▼
Entity building takes 2–3 months to reflect in AI models. Citation improvements show within weeks for real-time engines like Perplexity. Google AI Overviews respond fastest — often within days of technical optimizations.
Should I stop doing regular SEO?▼
No! SEO and GEO are complementary. Strong SEO actually helps GEO because AI models use web authority signals. We recommend doing both — but if you had to prioritize one new investment, GEO has the highest untapped potential right now.
Can you guarantee my brand will appear in ChatGPT?▼
No one can guarantee AI model outputs — they're non-deterministic. What we guarantee is that we'll implement every proven strategy to maximize your chances, and we'll measure and report on your visibility changes monthly.
How do you track AI search visibility?▼
We run systematic queries against ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, and Google AI Overviews monthly. We track whether your brand is cited, what competitors are mentioned, and how your visibility changes over time.

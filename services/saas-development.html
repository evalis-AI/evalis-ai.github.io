❓ FAQ

Common Questions

How long does it take to build a SaaS MVP?▼
A focused MVP with core features typically takes 8–12 weeks. Complex platforms with multiple user roles and integrations take 12–20 weeks. We'll give you an exact timeline after the discovery phase.
What tech stack do you use?▼
Our standard stack: React/Next.js (frontend), Node.js/Python (backend), PostgreSQL/Supabase (database), Stripe (billing), AWS/Vercel (hosting). We adapt based on your specific needs.
Do I own the code and IP?▼
100%. All source code, design files, database schemas, and documentation are yours. We transfer everything via GitHub. No lock-in, no licensing fees.
Can you help with post-launch?▼
Yes. We offer ongoing maintenance, feature development, and scaling support via monthly retainer agreements. Many of our SaaS clients have been with us for 1+ years.
I have a technical co-founder — do I still need you?▼
We work great alongside technical co-founders. We augment your team — handling frontend, backend, or infrastructure while your CTO focuses on product vision and architecture decisions.

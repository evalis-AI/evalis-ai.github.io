❓ FAQ

Common Questions

How long does a website take?▼
Simple websites take 2–3 weeks. Complex web apps with dashboards and auth take 4–8 weeks. We'll give you an exact timeline after understanding your requirements.
Do I own the code?▼
100%. All source code, design files, and assets are yours. We transfer the full repository to your GitHub/GitLab upon completion. No lock-in, ever.
Can you redesign my existing website?▼
Yes. We handle full redesigns, partial refreshes, and migrations from WordPress/Wix/Squarespace to custom-built solutions. We'll assess your current site and recommend the best approach.
Do you handle hosting?▼
We deploy your site to Vercel, Netlify, AWS, or your preferred hosting. We can manage hosting for you ($10–50/month) or hand off to your team with full documentation.

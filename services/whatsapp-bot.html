Yes! The bot can send and receive images, videos, PDFs, and location pins. It can share product photos, catalogs, and even video demos within the conversation.

Absolutely. The bot handles routine questions automatically, but you can take over any conversation at any time. When you're offline, the bot covers for you.

Since the bot is trained exclusively on YOUR data (using RAG architecture), it gives highly accurate, contextual answers. It doesn't make things up — if it doesn't know, it asks the customer to contact you directly.

❓ FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a white-label AI agent? ▼
A white-label AI agent is a custom chatbot branded to your business, trained on your data, and embedded on your website. It looks and feels like your own product — powered by Evalis AI infrastructure behind the scenes. Your customers never see "Evalis" unless you want them to.
How long does it take to build? ▼
We deliver your custom AI agent in 48 hours after receiving your business documents, FAQs, and branding assets. Complex enterprise setups with custom integrations may take 5–7 business days.
What languages does it support? ▼
English, Malayalam, Hindi, and 50+ other languages are supported out of the box via our multilingual LLM backend. Voice output is available in English and major Indian languages.
Can I update the agent's knowledge later? ▼
Yes! You can update your agent's knowledge base anytime — add new products, change pricing, update FAQs, or modify its personality. Changes are reflected within minutes.
Does it work on mobile? ▼
Absolutely. The agent widget is fully responsive and works beautifully on all devices — desktop, tablet, and mobile. It also supports voice input on Chrome and Edge browsers.
How does billing work? ▼
There's a one-time setup fee for training and deployment, plus a monthly hosting fee that covers infrastructure, AI model costs, and support. No hidden charges.

About Evalis AI

Human-Led AI Evaluation, Data Labeling & Search Quality Review

Who We Are

Evalis AI is an independent, human-led AI evaluation and data quality service focused on improving the accuracy, relevance, and reliability of artificial intelligence systems.

As AI models grow more powerful, their performance increasingly depends on high-quality human judgment. Evalis AI exists to bridge the gap between automated systems and real human understanding.

Our Philosophy

Unlike large-scale crowd platforms, Evalis AI follows a controlled and responsible evaluation approach.

  • No blind crowdsourcing
  • No automated-only scoring
  • No recycled or reused datasets

Every task is handled with context awareness, accuracy, and confidentiality. We evaluate AI systems the way real users experience them.

What We Do

  • AI Data Evaluation: Reviewing AI outputs for accuracy, usefulness, and intent alignment
  • Data Labeling & Annotation: High-precision annotation for search, NLP, and ML datasets
  • Search Engine Evaluation: Relevance testing, side-by-side comparison, and intent matching
  • Transcription & Linguistic QA: Speech-to-text quality review and error analysis
  • AI Output Quality & Safety Review: Detecting hallucinations, bias, and misinformation

Why Evalis AI

  • Human intelligence first – no guesswork
  • Independent and confidential workflow
  • Quality-focused, not volume-driven
  • Experience in search engine and AI evaluation tasks
  • Language expertise: English, Hindi, Malayalam

Who We Work With

Evalis AI supports AI startups, research teams, data science groups, and digital platforms that need accurate human validation before deploying AI systems.

Our Mission

To improve AI systems through responsible, accurate, and human-led evaluation, ensuring technology aligns with real-world human expectations and values.
